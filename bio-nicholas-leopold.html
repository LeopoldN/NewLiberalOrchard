Nicholas Leopold

Author / Founder of Liberal Orchard

Nicholas Leopold is currently pursuing his Master of Science in Economics and Politics at the University of Freiburg. With a Bachelor of Arts in Economics from Penn State University, he brings a strong foundation in economic theory and policy analysis. Nicholas is the founder of Liberal Orchard and is dedicated to informed discussions on economics, finance, and politics.

Inspired by the Freiburg School of Economics and the principles of Ordnungstheorie, Nicholas believes in a well-regulated, socially responsible market economy. His work advocates for a balanced approach to economic governance, aligning with the Clinton and Biden administrations' economic and political ideals. He is passionate about promoting policies that foster economic stability, fairness, and progress, aiming to contribute to meaningful change in today's complex world.
