It's November 6th and Donald Trump just got elected. We're going to recalibrate for the upcoming Trump Administration to highlight exactly why people were voting for him. (Hope you enjoy the new Artilce Photo) Therefore, the monthly review is getting an update as well as a change in topics, which include:

Consumer Prices: We're gonna focus on Egg, Milk, Bread, Meat, stock market, and gasoline prices.
Economic Data: Real GDP per Capita, Real Manufacturing Investment, CPI, oil output, housing data, and real wage data breakdown.
Jobs Data: Total Employment, Employment by Industry, and Laborforce Participation Rate.
Fiscal Policy: Average Effective Tax Rate, Cost of Tariffs, Total Debt, and Deficit.
Monetary Policy: Federal Funds Rate, Money Supply, and Exchange Rate
Quick Overview: A nice table that overviews all the data we've gone over.
